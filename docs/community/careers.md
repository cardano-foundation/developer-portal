Are you passionate about Cardano and looking for opportunities to build and contribute to the ecosystem? The Cardano community is growing rapidly, with numerous organizations and companies building innovative solutions on the blockchain.

Many opportunities in the Cardano ecosystem offer remote work options, allowing you to work from anywhere in the world with flexible schedules.

## Explore Companies Building on Cardano

The Cardano ecosystem includes hundreds of organizations spanning DeFi, NFTs, gaming, identity, supply chain, and more. Many of these companies are actively hiring developers, designers, and other technical roles.

To discover companies and projects in the Cardano ecosystem:

- [Browse Cardano Ecosystem Entities](https://cardano.org/entities) - Explore organizations building on Cardano across various categories
- [Cardano Developer Community](/docs/community/cardano-developer-community) - Connect with other developers and learn about opportunities

## Founding Entities

The Cardano ecosystem is supported by several founding entities that regularly offer career opportunities:

- [Cardano Foundation Careers](https://cardanofoundation.org/careers) - The Swiss-based foundation promoting Cardano adoption
- [EMURGO Careers](https://emurgo.io/careers/) - The official commercial arm of Cardano
- [Input Output (IOG) Careers](https://apply.workable.com/io-global/) - The research and engineering company behind Cardano's development

## Contributing to Open Source

Many Cardano projects are open source and welcome contributions from developers:

- Contribute to [Cardano Node](https://github.com/IntersectMBO/cardano-node)
- Build with [Cardano improvement proposals (CIPs)](https://cips.cardano.org/)
- Participate in [Project Catalyst](https://projectcatalyst.io/) - Cardano's innovation funding platform

## Developer Resources

Get started building on Cardano and showcase your skills:

- Follow our [Getting Started guides](/docs/get-started/)
- Learn about [Smart Contract development](/docs/build/smart-contracts/overview)
- Join the [Cardano Stack Exchange](https://cardano.stackexchange.com) to help others and demonstrate your expertise
