In this session of the Cardano Developer Office Hours, Max Grützmacher and Fabian Bormann from the Cardano Foundation’s Ecosystem Engineering team lead an open discussion on the future of wallet connections. They introduce the new Wallet Connector, explaining how it enhances dApp interactions and simplifies integration. The session covers upcoming changes for developers, ongoing challenges, and how community feedback is helping shape new features and improvements across the Cardano ecosystem.
